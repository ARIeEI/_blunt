FLOX

Oxfam New Zealand, Blunt Umbrellas and Kiwi artist Flox have collaborated on a limited edition ‘Flox for Blunt’ umbrella to help raise interest in, and funds, for Oxfam NZ’s climate change initiatives in the Pacific region.

MICHAEL C HSUING

Y&R has collaborated with LA artist Michael C Hsiung to produce a series of posters to market Blunt Umbrellas. Various characters are rained on from above – not by water – but by objects that would otherwise cause them displeasure or comedic harm.Y&R Associate Creative Director Tom Paine said “Michael is a disturbingly talented illustrator that I’ve wanted to work with for many years. The planets aligned on this one,and we’re looking forward to the next.”
